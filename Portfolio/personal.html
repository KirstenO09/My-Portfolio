hobbies, interest, and courses I took at Wayne State University.

I developed a personal website using HTML and CSS to showcase my education, interests, hobbies, and coursework at Wayne State University. The website features a clean and modern design, incorporating thoughtful typography, color schemes, and layout structures to ensure a user-friendly experience. It serves as a digital portfolio, highlighting my academic background in Information Technology, the courses that shaped my skills, and my passion for web development and design. Additionally, I included sections that reflect my personal interests and hobbies, allowing visitors to gain insight into who I am beyond my technical abilities. This project not only demonstrates my proficiency in front-end development but also allows me to creatively express my personality and professional journey through web design.
